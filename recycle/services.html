About CLEANSE

The study focuses on developing a web-based waste management with a rewards system to promote recycling in the midst of pandemic. Our services varies depending on what waste you have and what you need. We can make things work for you and your wastes. This includes the following:

Waste Pickup

We will pick-up your waste at your chosen pick-up location and deliver it to your chosen waste management establishment

Coaching

We will help you know more about your waste. We will teach you on how to properly dispose them and whether we accept them.

Proper Segregation

This web application aims to help the users to participate in proper waste segregation.

Redeem Points

Users will be able to collect rewards points from every pick-up or delivery. This points have an equivalent rewards.

Accepted Waste

CLEANSE accepts the following waste items:

Plastic Bottles

Cleanse collects plastic bottles. This include plastic softdrinks bottles, water bottles, condiment bottles and the likes. Make sure to empty out any liquids from the bottle before turning over for the collection. No need to crush/flatten the bottles.

Paper

Cleanse collects papers. This includes white paper, bond paper, used office paper, news paper and the likes. White paper printed or written with black ink will be grouped with other white papers, while those papers with heavy prints will be separated ass well as newspapers. Papers with sensitive information can should be shredded.

Cartons

Cleanse collects cartons and boxes. This includes corrugated cardboard boxes, paper bags and the likes. Flatten all boxes and stack it together. You can also cut or fold large flattened boxes into smaller size if possible

Cans

Cleanse collects cans. This includes aluminum cans like canned softdrinks or canned beer, and tin cans like the ones used in ready-to-go food cans. Make sure that the cans are clean and dry before turning over for collection. Pack the cans with other aluminum or tin items.
